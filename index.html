Globe to Map

Draw on the globe and export a 2D equirectangular map.

(I recommend you turn on "Use hardware acceleration when available" on your browser. It may also be called "Use graphics acceleration when available".)

Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys to rotate the globe. The current coordinates of the center are printed on the upper left (Latitude Longitude order).
  • Drag the mouse to draw on the globe.
  • Press 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 or E to draw in either Red, Yellow, Green, Cyan, Blue, Magenta, or Erase.
  • Press W or S to zoom in or out.
  • Press M to switch in and out of "Map Mode". You cannot draw in "Map Mode".
  • To export an image, simply right click and save the image.
